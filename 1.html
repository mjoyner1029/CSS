Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream the butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar together until light and fluffy.
  3. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  4. Mix in the flour, baking soda, and salt until well combined.
  5. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  6. Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto baking sheets.
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until golden brown.
  8. Let the cookies cool on the baking sheets for a few minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
